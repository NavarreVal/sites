Miles Wireless provides high speed, low cost internet access.  We do wireless linking from our base to your home.  We don't use a phone line to provide this service but place a radio on your roof and run a wire into your home to give you an internet connection.

 

Miles Wireless covers the Pleasant Grove Utah area.  Because all connections must be in line of site to one of our three bases, we provide a free site survey to let you know if we can provide internet service to your home.

Content Filtering

Content Filtering will screen and exclude access and availability to pages that contain objectionable content, and can be a very useful tool for combating negative influences in the household.  Content Filtering can be a powerful tool to keep your computer, and your family, safe.

 

If you would like to filter what can get into your home on the Internet, we provide a free content filter proxy. It blocks pages that have too many bad words and can also block web pages that are known to be "bad sites."
